Vendors

Become a Vendor

Vendor spaces are available for $100 per slot. This includes up to 4 company representatives, one 4' x 6' table and two chairs. Space under the pavilion is limited and available on a first come first serve basis. Competing vendors will be allowed (provided you do not compete with Businets directly). We will try to place competing vendors apart from each other as availability allows. Businets reserves the right to deny vendor participation.

Non-Profits: Reduced charge $30 - Non-Profit space is limited to 10 spaces under the pavilion. If these spots are spoken for you could pay regular price for an available vendor slot.
